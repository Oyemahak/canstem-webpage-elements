CAEC vs OSSD Side-by-side comparison of the Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD) and CAEC high school equivalency.
OSSD (Ontario Secondary School Diploma)
  • High School Diploma accepted by all Ontario Colleges and Universities.
  • Accepted worldwide for study and immigration purposes.
  • Includes a full high school transcript with course-by-course grades.
  • Meets admission requirements for competitive and degree programs.
  • Issued by an inspected Ontario high school (Ministry-approved curriculum).
