Payment Confirmation

We provide receipts for all payments, and you also receive e-transfer confirmations directly in your email.

  • If, for any reason, you require all payment details on our official letterhead, we can provide a consolidated confirmation letter at a nominal cost.
  • This cost covers the time and resources required to review your payment transactions and compile them into a single official payment confirmation document.
  • The fee varies depending on the number of transactions involved, when the payments were made, and the payment methods used.
  • You can contact us and submit your request with specific requirements, and we will get back to you with the applicable fee for the confirmation letter and the expected delivery timeline.
