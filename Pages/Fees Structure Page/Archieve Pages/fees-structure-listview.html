Course mode change

You may change your course within one week of registration, provided you have not started or accessed the course (in-class or online). After one week, fees will apply based on the number of units accessed:

  • Course not accessed after one week: You will need to pay $75.
  • One unit accessed after one week: You will need to pay $75 + $50 (and so on for each additional unit).

Course change- You may change your course within one week of registration if you have not started or accessed the course (in-class or online). After one week, a fee will apply for switching course mode or course. We reserve the right to refuse your request for a change in course or mode of course. For more details, please contact us at 647-847-1231 or frontdesk@canstemeducation.com
