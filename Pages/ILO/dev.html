About the International Logic Olympiad (ILO)

What is ILO?

The International Logic Olympiad is a global logic competition hosted annually in partnership with Stanford University. It is designed for students who enjoy reasoning challenges and want to demonstrate excellence in logic and problem-solving.

What the contest tests

  • Logical reasoning and careful deduction
  • Pattern recognition and structured thinking
  • Symbolic and set-based reasoning (union, intersection, complement)
  • Multi-step problems that reward clarity over memorization

Official pages and resources: logicolympiad.org • Official Sample Test

Our ILO Coaching Program (How we teach)

Our coaching is built around ILO-style practice, clear methods, and timed performance training. Students learn how to solve efficiently while still showing clean reasoning.

What’s included

  • Diagnostic check + a clear plan (strengths, gaps, pacing)
  • Weekly training sets with guided solution methods
  • Reasoning drills: deductions, constraints, and proof-style thinking
  • Mock tests (timed) + mistake breakdown and improvement plan
  • Support for both Junior and Senior divisions

Learning options

  • In-person coaching in Brampton
  • Online live, interactive coaching (1-on-1 or small group)
  • Flexible scheduling with hourly or monthly plans
