What is ILO, and what is CanSTEM’s role?

The International Logic Olympiad (ILO) is a global logic competition designed to promote logic education and celebrate high-level reasoning. CanSTEM Education is an official ILO Regional Partner (Ontario, Canada)—supporting students with coaching, planning, and local guidance.

What ILO tests

Logical deduction, careful reading, patterns, sets, and multi-step reasoning—designed to reward clarity over memorization.

  • Logic puzzles & constraints
  • Pattern recognition
  • Set reasoning (union/intersection/complement)
  • Structured, step-by-step thinking

CanSTEM & ILO

As an official Regional Partner (Ontario, Canada), CanSTEM supports families with a clear pathway from practice to performance.

  • Coaching + mock tests
  • Division placement guidance
  • Test-day readiness support
  • Online coaching across Canada

Why students love it

ILO is exciting because it feels like “smart puzzles” with a competitive edge—perfect for students who like challenges.

  • Fun, brainy problem sets

  • Calm execution under time

Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

Are you an official ILO Regional Partner?
Yes. CanSTEM Education supports ILO as an official Regional Partner (Ontario, Canada). We help students prepare with coaching and guide families through official steps and readiness.
Where can we try official ILO-style questions?
Use the official sample test here: portal.logicolympiad.org/sample. We also train students using sample-style sets with timing and strategy.
What are the key dates for 2026?
Registration opens Jan 14, 2026 and closes Mar 18, 2026. Preliminary rounds are in April (Junior Apr 18, Senior Apr 25). Finals are July 1–3, 2026.
What is the registration fee?
The official ILO registration fee is $50 (paid through the official portal). Coaching fees are separate.
Do you offer online coaching across Canada?
Yes. We provide live, interactive online sessions for students across Canada, plus in-person coaching in Brampton.
How do we start?
Submit the inquiry form with the student’s grade and goals. We’ll recommend a plan and schedule, then start weekly training and mock exams closer to the preliminaries.

Ready to start ILO preparation?

A strong ILO score comes from consistent training: clean methods, smart pacing, and confidence under time. Start with the official sample test, then book coaching so your child improves fast and feels ready.
