What is the Advanced Placement (AP) Program? +

The AP Program offers official university-level courses and exams for high school students. Each AP course follows a College Board course outline and is linked to an AP Exam (scored 1-5). Many universities use AP scores to grant advanced standing or credit.

Is CanSTEM an official AP Exam test centre? +

Not at this time. CanSTEM is a Level 1 partner that provides AP-aligned courses and full exam preparation. Students write their AP Exams at a College Board-authorized test centre in their region (often another AP school or testing centre).

We guide families on when and where to register for AP Exams locally.

Can I stay at my current school and still take AP with CanSTEM? +

Yes. Most students remain at their public, Catholic, or private school and take AP courses online with CanSTEM in the evenings or on a schedule that works for them.

You do not need to transfer schools to benefit from AP classes and exam prep with us.

Can international students take AP courses with CanSTEM? +

Yes. Our AP courses are fully online, so international students can join from anywhere. You will attend virtual classes, complete work on our platform, and then register for AP Exams at a test centre in your country or region.

Which AP courses should I choose for my future program? +

Engineering / Computer Science: AP Calculus AB or BC, AP Physics (1, 2 or C), AP Computer Science A or AP CSP.

Health sciences / medicine: AP Biology, AP Chemistry, AP Statistics, AP Psychology.

Business / economics / commerce: AP Macroeconomics, AP Microeconomics, AP Statistics, AP Calculus AB.

During your enrollment call, we help you build the best AP combination for your goals and schedule.

How many AP courses should I take at once? +

Many students start with one AP course in their strongest subject. Very strong students might handle two or three, depending on their other courses and activities.

We review your timetable and recommend a realistic AP course load so you can succeed without burning out.
