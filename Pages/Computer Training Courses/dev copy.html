Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

Who should join Level 1?

Level 1 is for absolute beginners—Grade 5–6 students and adults who are new to computers or want to build confidence with typing, files, email and safe browsing.

Can I skip a level?

Yes. We can run a 5–10 minute skills check and place you at the best starting level.

Do you offer online classes?

Yes. All levels are offered in-class in Brampton and live online via video meet. Materials are shared through Google Drive or OneDrive.

Schedule & Duration

When do batches start?

We start new groups every 2–3 weeks. Contact us to join the next available batch.

How many hours per week?

Most learners attend 2–4 hours weekly (evenings/weekends). Private or accelerated options are available.

Course Content

Which Microsoft 365 and Google apps are covered?

Across the five levels you’ll use Word/Docs, Excel/Sheets, PowerPoint/Slides, Outlook/Gmail, Calendar, Forms, Drive/OneDrive and collaboration features. Automation appears in Level 5.

Do we learn website creation with AI?

Yes. Level 5 covers AI site builders (Wix AI, Durable AI, Framer AI, Hostinger AI), branding, customization, and launch.

Is coding included?

Level 3 introduces HTML basics and Scratch mini projects. Level 4 adds responsive design and JavaScript fundamentals. Level 5 includes WordPress (PHP) and full builds.

Certificates & Outcomes

Do I get a certificate?

Yes. You receive a certificate after each level. Level 5 ends with a capstone website launch you can showcase.

Will this help with jobs or co-op?

Levels 3–5 focus on workplace skills. We include resume building, LinkedIn setup and project artifacts to show employers.
