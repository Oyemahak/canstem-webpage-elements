Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

Who should join Level 1?

Level 1 is designed for absolute beginners—students in Grade 5–6 and adults who are new to computers. If you can turn on a computer but feel unsure with files, typing or email, start here.

Can I skip a level?

Yes. We offer a short skills check. If you already know Level 1 topics, you can begin at Level 2 or higher.

Do you offer online classes?

Yes. All levels are available in-class in Brampton and live online via video meet. Materials are shared through Google Drive or OneDrive.

Schedule & Duration

When do the batches start?

We run rolling batches. New groups typically start every 2–3 weeks. Contact us to see the next start date that suits your schedule.

How many hours per week?

Most learners attend 2–4 hours per week on weekday evenings or weekends. Private and accelerated options are also available.

Course Content

Which Microsoft 365 and Google apps are covered?

Across the five levels you will learn Word/Docs, Excel/Sheets, PowerPoint/Slides, Outlook/Gmail, Calendar, Forms, Drive/OneDrive, and collaboration features. Advanced features and automation are covered in Levels 4–5.

Do we learn website creation with AI?

Yes. Level 5 includes AI website builders such as Wix AI, Durable AI, Framer AI and Hostinger AI, plus customization, branding and launch steps.

Is there coding?

Level 3 introduces HTML basics and Scratch mini projects. Level 4 covers responsive design and JavaScript fundamentals. Level 5 focuses on full builds and WordPress with PHP.

Certificates & Outcomes

Do I get a certificate?

Yes. You receive a completion certificate for each level. Level 5 concludes with a portfolio or business website launch as a capstone.

Will this help with jobs or co-op?

Levels 3–5 are designed for workplace skills. We include resume building, LinkedIn setup, and project artifacts you can show to employers.
