Awards & Recognition

“Top 10 School Principals in Canada 2026”

Recognition by Education Insider Canada celebrates leadership, innovation, and a student-centred approach.

We’re thrilled to share inspiring news with our community: CanSTEM Education Private School’s Principal, Sajjala P. Sankhe, has been shortlisted by Education Insider Canada as one of the “Top 10 School Principals in Canada 2026.” This national recognition highlights our leader’s outstanding work in educational administration, advancing academic excellence, and cultivating a student-centred learning environment across CanSTEM Education Private School.

About the Recognition

Education Insider Canada is a prominent magazine reaching 98,000+ qualified print and digital subscribers—including senior executives, school leaders, and education decision-makers. Known for spotlighting both technological and non-technological advancements, the publication provides deep insights, analysis, and narratives that empower educators, students, administrators, and education enthusiasts.

How selection works: The shortlist is based on reader nominations and a comprehensive evaluation conducted by the magazine’s advisory board, emphasizing measurable impact on student development and institutional performance.

Why It Matters for Students and Families

This recognition strengthens CanSTEM’s position as an institution that champions student achievement, invests in faculty excellence, and sets high benchmarks for academic programs & high school credits. It reflects the systems we’ve built for OSSD planning, transparent assessment & reporting, and the flexible pathways students rely on—from subject tutoring and IELTS/CELPIP/PTE coaching to Online High School and Adult Education.
