What is the difference between CanSTEM and other private schools, online and in-person schools?

A clear, side-by-side explanation—kept simple, accurate, and student-focused.

At CanSTEM Education Private School, we combine the reliability of an Ontario private school with the flexibility of an online high school. Below are the exact ways we’re different, compared with other private and public schools in Ontario.

Real-time teachers, 5 days a week

Students get live teacher support to ask questions and discuss the course in real time. Many other schools rely on prerecorded classes or just upload PowerPoints/notes—leaving students without a teacher to turn to.

7-day customer support

Call, text, or email—our team replies quickly, 7 days a week.

Same-week report cards

We process and release report cards the same week you complete your final exam—in some cases, the same day.

Fast grading

Grading typically happens the same week—often within 1–2 business days. Report cards go out as soon as grading is done (same day or within ~1–2 days).

OUAC/OCAS uploads & transcripts

We upload grades to OUAC, OCAS, and send transcripts/report cards to universities and colleges within 2–4 business days. Other schools often take weeks.

Flexible payments

Installment options + e-transfer, in-person, debit, credit, PayPal, cash, and account transfers. Many schools don’t offer installments or PayPal/in-person options.

Brampton campus—walk in for help

Students can walk in during business hours for extra teaching support—no charge, no appointment. We also arrange on-demand Zoom teaching sessions.

Final exams on campus (free) or online

Write your final exam at our campus without a proctor and at no cost—or choose our secure online, no-proctor exam option.

Real in-person support

We have a real campus where students and families can visit for help, guidance, and support. Some schools don’t allow in-person visits and take weeks to respond.

1,370 five-star reviews (4.9 Google rating)

We’re a 10-year-old school with a strong reputation for consistent, reliable service and student support—see our reviews.

More courses than other schools

We offer a wider selection than most online or in-person private/public schools in Ontario—explore High School Credits, STEM Programs, Healthcare Programs, Adult Education, and more.
