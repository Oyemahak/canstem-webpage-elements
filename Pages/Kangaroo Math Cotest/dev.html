About the Math Kangaroo Contest

Math Kangaroo Contest (Grades 1–12) — Brampton overview

The Canadian Math Kangaroo Contest is known for fun and challenging questions that reward creative thinking—not just memorized formulas. Students typically face multiple-choice problems designed to build confidence in logic, patterns, and problem-solving.

  • Grades: 1–12
  • Skills tested: logical reasoning, number sense, geometry, and smart strategies
  • Great for: students who love puzzles, want a first contest experience, or aim for awards

We can also prepare students for related contests such as Waterloo math contests and other school math/science competitions. Also, we can suggest a plan based on your child’s level.

Benefits of Math Kangaroo Preparation

What students gain (beyond the contest)

  • Sharper thinking: Learn how to break down unfamiliar questions quickly.
  • Better school performance: Stronger foundations in number sense, geometry, and reasoning.
  • Confidence under time: Practice with timed sets and mock tests.
  • Healthy challenge: Students build resilience and learn from mistakes.
  • Recognition: Many students aim for certificates, ribbons, and medals through strong performance.

Parents often tell us the biggest difference is not just higher scores—it's how students start enjoying challenging questions and become comfortable with “contest-style” thinking. Therefore, they feel more confident on test day.

Our Coaching Program (How we teach)

Our coaching is structured around contest-style problem sets, guided solutions, and weekly progress support. Students learn both strategy and accuracy.

What’s included

  • Level-appropriate topic review (without over-teaching)
  • Contest-style worksheets + timed mini tests
  • Step-by-step solution methods and shortcuts
  • Error analysis: “why this answer is wrong” training
  • Q&A support with experienced instructors

Class options

  • In-person coaching in Brampton
  • Online live, interactive coaching (1-on-1 or small group)
  • Flexible scheduling for hourly or monthly packages

Also, we can adjust pacing as needed. As a result, students stay challenged without feeling overwhelmed.
