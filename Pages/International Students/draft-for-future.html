International Students — Fees, PLAR & Required Documents

If you are applying to CanSTEM Education Private School as an international student (online or in-person), please first review our main International Student page . That page explains eligibility, document requirements, and how we process international transcripts.

1. Enrollment & Required Documents

To start your application, you should be ready with the following:

  • Student’s passport / government-issued photo ID (copy)
  • Most recent report card / transcript from your home country
  • Proof of current grade level or schooling status
  • Parent / guardian contact details (for minors)
  • Any previous English / French course records, if available

For immigration-related admissions (Letter of Acceptance / full-time), we may request additional documents or ask for full annual fees up front. Our staff will confirm this after you submit your form.

2. Taking Only a Few Ontario Courses (Individual Credit Courses)

If you only want 1-3 Ontario high school courses (for example, ENG4U, MHF4U, MCV4U, SBI4U), you do not need to apply as a full-time student. Instead:

  • Go to our website and click “Enroll Now.”
  • Select “High School Credit Course.”
  • When the form asks about your status, choose the option for International student.
Item Amount Notes
Transcript Fee $250 (one-time) Applies once, even if you take multiple courses.
PLAR (Prior Learning Assessment) $150 per subject stream One PLAR for each subject stream (English, Math, Science, etc.). After PLAR is approved, you can continue in that stream without paying PLAR again.
Assessment Charges $450 For academic placement / course mapping.
Admin Fee $95 One-time processing.
Documentation Processing Fee $250 For international document verification.
Consultation Fee $100 For academic planning and next steps.
Course Fee — Grade 9-10 $449 per course Online or in-class, Ontario credit.
Course Fee — Grade 11-12 $499 per course University / college preparation credits.

Example (English stream): If you studied Grade 10 English in your country, you pay $150 PLAR for ENG2D (Ontario Grade 10 English). Once that is approved, you can take ENG3U (Grade 11) and then ENG4U (Grade 12) without paying PLAR again for English. Only one PLAR is charged per stream.

3. Full-Time OSSD Program (Online or In-Class)

Students who want to study full-time and complete the Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD) must pay the one-time registration fee and annual tuition for their grade.

Grade 9 $7,350 per year Full-time tuition.
Grade 10 $7,350 per year Full-time tuition.
