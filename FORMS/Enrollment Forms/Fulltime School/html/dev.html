Proof of Education

  • For Secondary students who are attending or have attended secondary school in Ontario, please provide the most recent transcript, report card or credit summary report (if available).
  • Courses in Grades 10, 11, and 12 may have prerequisites for enrolment. For instance, student must have done grade 11 functions (MCR3U) in order to enroll in grade 12 Advanced Functions (MHF4U). Click here to learn more about Course Descriptions and Pre-Requisites.
  • Student must submit one the following documents below, or no midterm or final grades will be released and no enrollment letter or confirmation will be issued.

Proof of Age

Upload a copy of one original document from the list below

  • Canadian Birth Certificate/Birth Registration Card.
  • Canadian Citizenship Card / Certificate / Passport.
  • Permanent Resident Card / Confirmation of Permanent Residence.
  • Work permit/Employment Authorization from Citizenship and Immigration Canada.
  • Study Permit issued to parent for a diploma or degree program from Citizenship and Immigration Canada.
  • Refugee/Convention Refugee Permit.

Proof of Address

Upload a copy of one original document from the list below

  • Ontario Driver's License.
  • Utility Bill (water, hydro, gas, phone, cable, cell phone).
  • Bank Statement/Letter from Financial Institution.
  • Credit Card Statement.
  • Government forms (i.e. Social Insurance Number, Service Canada documents).
  • Purchase Agreement.

Proof of Immunization

Students registering in an Ontario school for the first time must provide proof of immunization/vaccination. Students with an Ontario Education Number (shown on Ontario report cards or transcripts) do not need to provide proof of immunization.

Immunization of School Pupils Act, R.S.O. 1990, c. I.1

Letter from the Ministry of Health

Proof of Custody

Children must live with their parent(s) unless provided documentation supports an alternate living arrangement.
