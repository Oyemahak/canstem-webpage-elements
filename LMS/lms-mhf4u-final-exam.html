Final Exam & Report Card Information

The final exam counts for 30% of your course grade.

Before You Write

  • Submit all required coursework (assignments, quizzes, tests) and maintain active participation.
  • Add CanSTEM to your OUAC/OCAS account.
  • Clear any pending fees and provide required prerequisite transcripts/report cards.
  • If writing in person, visit the school during business hours (bring valid photo ID and your own stationery).

Exam Format

Within 100 km of Brampton campus

  • Exam is in person at school (proctored).
  • No smart devices allowed (phones, tablets, smartwatches, programmable calculators, etc.).
  • No re-attempts once the exam has started.
