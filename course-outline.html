📘 MHF4U Course Outline & Timeline

This Grade 12 University Preparation course builds a strong foundation in advanced functions and prepares students for Calculus and university-level mathematics. Students explore polynomial, exponential, logarithmic, rational, and trigonometric functions, developing problem-solving strategies and mathematical thinking required for postsecondary success.

Unit Order Unit Name Suggested Time
Unit 1 Exponential and Logarithmic Functions 25 Hours
Unit 2 Trigonometric Functions 30 Hours
Unit 3 Polynomial and Rational Functions 25 Hours
Unit 4 Characteristics of Functions 25 Hours
Final Exam and Summative 5 Hours
Total 110 Hours

🧠 Teaching & Learning Strategies

The course includes direct instruction, class discussions, independent work, worksheets, quizzes, projects, and real-world problem solving. Interactive and inquiry-based methods are used to foster deeper understanding and critical thinking.

📝 Assessment & Evaluation

Students are assessed through a mix of assignments, quizzes, tests, and a final evaluation. Here's how grading is structured:

  • ✔ 70% – Term Work (Knowledge, Thinking, Application, Communication)
  • ✔ 30% – Final Evaluation (Exam + Summative Task)

📊 Breakdown by Category:

  • 📘 Knowledge & Understanding – 20%
  • 💡 Thinking & Inquiry – 20%
  • 🛠 Application – 40%
  • 🗣 Communication – 20%

📎 Additional Course Policies

  • 📌 Minimum enrollment of 2 weeks for midterm and 4 weeks for final eligibility.
  • 📌 Late work: initial warning → 2 mark deduction → parent-principal meeting.
  • 📌 Plagiarism or cheating = automatic zero and incident logged.
  • 📌 Attendance is mandatory. Absences must be informed by 9:00 AM.
