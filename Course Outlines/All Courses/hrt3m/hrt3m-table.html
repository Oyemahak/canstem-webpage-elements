Unit Title Expectations Summary Time
1 Religious Beliefs Study key religious histories, compare core beliefs, explore sacred texts/oral teachings, and understand rituals and symbols. 30 Hours
2 Social Structures Explore religion’s role in civilizations, art, and gender roles, and understand religious diversity in Canada. 25 Hours
3 Religion and the Human Experience Examine why religion matters, address misconceptions, analyze historical figures, and consider religion’s future impact. 30 Hours
4 Research and Inquiry Skills Build research skills using surveys/interviews/primary sources, evaluate evidence, and communicate findings clearly. 20 Hours
Final Final Exam Written exam (30%). Term work across Units 1–4 counts for 70% of the final grade. 5 Hours
Total Course Total HRT3M (Grade 11) is a 1.00 credit Ontario course with no prerequisite. 110 Hours
