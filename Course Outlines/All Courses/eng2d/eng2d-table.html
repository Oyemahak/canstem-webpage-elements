Unit Title Expectations Summary Time
1 Short Stories Read widely and analyze short fiction. Then, complete writing tasks and craft an original short story using imagination and structure. 24 Hours
2 Poetry Study poetic forms and devices (stanzas, rhyme, imagery). Also, write your own poems and finish with a poetry anthology. 20 Hours
3 Shakespeare Drama Explore Shakespeare’s work and key drama terms while analyzing themes and structure. Oral communication is emphasized through readings and presentations. 25 Hours
4 Novel Study Use literary terminology to analyze an assigned novel. In addition, research context, practise citations, and avoid plagiarism in written work. 25 Hours
5 Media and Culminating Unit Study media forms and create media products such as a tabloid, newscast, magazine, or storyboard. Focus on the relationship between form and content. 15 Hours
Total Course Total Total instructional time for ENG2D. 110 Hours
