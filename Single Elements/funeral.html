About Mr. Parag Madhukar Deorukhkar

Parag Madhukar Deorukhkar, who comes from a humble background, was born, raised, and educated in Mumbai, India. He pursued excellence in his academic journey, earning a Master of Engineering from the University of Mumbai, India, and another Master of Engineering from Newcastle University, USA.

Parag distinguished himself professionally, contributing his talents to multinational organizations such as Mitsubishi, Nokia, CamTech, Zim, and Coral. In Canada, he developed pioneering applications for Pitney Bowes and the NHL, leaving a lasting mark on the technology landscape.

A devoted educator, Parag shared his expertise by teaching MS to countless students over the years. Beyond his professional accomplishments, he was a loving husband to Sajjala Sankhe and a doting father to Dr. Aditi Sankhe, who carries forward his intellectual legacy in the service of science and humanity, Canada.
