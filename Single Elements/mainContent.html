The OUAC collects and updates marks only for students applying to at least one university within Ontario. However, the OUAC also assists the following out-of-province universities in collecting academic data for Ontario high school students who have applied to at least one Ontario university through the OUAC:
    • Acadia University
    • Bishop's University
    • The University of British Columbia
    • Concordia University
    • Dalhousie University
    • McGill University
    • Saint Mary's University
    • Simon Fraser University
    • The University of Victoria
Students applying to these out-of-province universities can authorize them to request academic information from the OUAC. To do so, applicants must include their 11-digit OUAC Reference Number on the university's application form, where the 11th digit is always "0."
