Grade 9 Courses

AVI1OVisual Arts
Prerequisite: None
Course Outline:
This course focuses on studio activities in one or more of the visual arts, including drawing, painting, sculpture, photography, printmaking, and multimedia art. Students explore the elements and principles of design while developing technical skills and creative expression. They study works of art from various cultures and historical periods, analyzing artistic choices and techniques.
ADA1ODrama

Course Outline:
This course provides opportunities for students to explore dramatic forms and techniques, using material from various sources. Students work individually and collaboratively to devise, interpret, and present drama works. They examine issues and themes in drama and explore techniques that enhance dramatic presentations. Students also analyze and critique their own work and that of others.
BEM1OBuilding The Entrepreneurial Mindset

This course introduces students to entrepreneurship and the entrepreneurial mindset. Students learn about different types of businesses and examine the characteristics of successful entrepreneurs. They explore the creative process, develop their own business ideas, and learn about planning and launching a business venture. The course emphasizes innovation, problem-solving, and business ethics.

Topics include natural resource management, climate change, urban development, and sustainable practices.
